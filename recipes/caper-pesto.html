Caper Pesto

<- Back to Menu

Description

This fresh, herby pesto combines basil, dill, and capers with a bright twist of lemon and Parmesan for a zesty, aromatic spread. Perfect for pasta, sandwiches, or as a dip, this pesto puts a briny twist on a timeless Italian sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups packed fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup packed fresh dill
  • 3 tablespoons capers
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon zest
  • 2 cloves smashed garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Process basil, Parmesan, oil, pine nuts, green onions, dill, capers, juice, zest, garlic, salt, and pepper in a food processor until nearly smooth
  3. Chill in an airtight container up to 1 week; freeze up to 3 months.
